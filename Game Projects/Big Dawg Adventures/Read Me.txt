Big Dawg Adventures is the capstone project of a two semester long software engineering course at Western Illinois University.
It was the collaborative effort of Dylan Gibbs, Jason Strack, and Lawson Meredith. 
This game has been coded entirely in Java, using RyiSno's RPG series as a jumping off point.

The game itself is an RPG inspired by the original Legend of Zelda. 
It has an overworld, dungeons, and submaps to explore.
It has different items to change the style of play.

Fight Slimes, Robo-Dogs, and The monsterous Cyber-Cat to save the town!
